Unit 5: Trigonometry Geometry

Lesson 3: Solving Trig Equations

The unit circle, or trig circle as it's also known, is useful to know
because it lets us easily calculate the cosine, sine, and tangent of
any angle between 0° and 360°. In a Unit Circle, the cos value of an
angle will be the x value of the coordinate. While the sin value
is the y coordinate of the angle. Which means the tan value
is the x coordinate divided by the y coordinate.
