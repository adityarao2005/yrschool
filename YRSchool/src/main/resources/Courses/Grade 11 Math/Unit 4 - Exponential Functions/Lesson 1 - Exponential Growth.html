Whats the trend?
Its all repeating the differences, and its increasing its value by multiplying two. Lets make an equation 
 y = 100(2)x
This is the equation for the bacteria, it is a type of exponential growth. But this doesnt account for any exponential growth. Lets make the general formula, like from functions transformations.
From: 
 y = af(dx - e) + c
The equation is: 
 y = a(bdx - e) + c
Where:
  • a is the coefficient to the exponent
  • b is the base of the exponent and b < 1
  • c is the vertical translation of the asymptote
  • d is the coefficient of x
  • e is the horizontal translation of x
  • f(x) = bx
